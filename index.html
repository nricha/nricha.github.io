Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Richa Naniwadekar and I’m a budding graphic designer in the Bay Area. My passion to find creativity in everything sparked my interest in graphic design. My strengths lie in digital illustration, logo design, branding, building corporate identity, application and web design.

What I do

I am currently pursuing an A.A in Graphic Design from De Anza College (expected graduation: Summer 2017) and looking forward to enhance my design skills in the field. I am a travel bug and grab every opportunity I can to plan a trip!
