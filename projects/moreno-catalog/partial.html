Gabriel Moreno Catalog

Goal: To design a high-end museum catalog for a famous artist. The catalog had to include multiple spreads, museum logo, front and back cover as well as a biography page, table of contents and three additional pages displaying the artistic plates. Even though the museum branding had to be the same, the goal was to incorporate the museum logo and information on the back cover and inside the catalog. The art plate spreads had to embrace negative space.
