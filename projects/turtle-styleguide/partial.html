Save a turtle style guide

Goal: To design a corporate branding kit for an existing mid-sized non-profit organization. The aim was to establish a design direction for the company by exploring possibilities through research. We had to re-brand the logo and define the various systems like color, type, collateral, grid/ layout and image system. This style guide would represent the corporate identity and provide guidelines for appropriate usage of logo, images or other materials related to the company.
