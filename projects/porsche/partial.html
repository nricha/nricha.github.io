Porche

Goal: To create a vector illustration of a vehicle. The vehicle had to look realistic with high amount of details including the part lines. In order to achieve a sense of realism, the project was expected to be created using various tools in Adobe Illustrator like pencil, pen, mesh, gradients, blends and pathfinder. The vehicle had to be based on an existing high quality car image which would be used to compare the final digital illustration outcome with.
