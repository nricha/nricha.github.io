Cherry Blossom Festival

Goal: To create a visually interesting poster design for a city festival. The poster had to be based on an actual event and required redesigning the logo and adding vector illustrations with a strong flat graphic look and feel. The poster also had to show event information like title, place, line up, dates and times. Along with making the poster attractive, it had to have a use good composition and visual hierarchy. The event graphics were to be populated on multiple event materials too.
