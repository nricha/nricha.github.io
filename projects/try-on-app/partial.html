try On app

Goal: To design a tablet application based on the concept of a designers tool which can be used as a problem solver. The application had to lean heavily on form, user experience and have a clear sense of function and logic to it. The design had to be simple and user friendly. It had to include five sequential screens with the purpose of moving the user to the end goal. Social media had to be woven into the design as it would serve as a sharing feature.
Ipad mock-up
Import a Logo
Choose a logo for your mockup from a variety of sources: Dropdox, Google Drive and your iPad's Photo Gallery
Select an item
Select an item to place your logo on. Try-on supports a variety of common objects: t-shirts, mugs, baseball caps, bottles and boxes.
Create Your Design
Position your logo on the chosen item. Grid-lines with snapping help you position the logo accurately. Try a variety of colors. Drag and swipe to rotate the object or snap view to pre-defined views (front, back, left and right). You can also add text to be placed on the item.
Preview
Save your creation or share it on social media. Optionally, purchase it from vendors such as Teespring by placing an order with them.
Create an Account
If you choose to save your creation, create a Try-on account so save it to. Or sign in if you already have an account.
