Knots and Weaves

Goal: To design a website and navigation system for an existing small scale business. The website had to be responsive on desktop, tablet and phone displaying the information accurately on either displays while keeping the design direction uniform. The different aspect ratios had to maintain the functionality, branding and important content displayed. The company had to be re-branded by redesigning the logo and changing the use of colors and type treatment.
