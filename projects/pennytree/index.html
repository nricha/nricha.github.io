Pennytree Personal Finance

Pennytree is an app that helps users save money quickly in preparation for a big purchase or expense. It is designed to provide a unique and personal experience with features like personalized advice, notifications, timeline, tracking finances, creating goals, and adding transactions. The app aims to help motivate users to reach their goals within a specific timeframe. It is equally easy to use for personal finance novices and experts.
