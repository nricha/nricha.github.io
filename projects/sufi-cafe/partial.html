Sufi Cafe

Goal: To design a logo, letterhead, envelope and double sided business card for a contemporary company of our choice. While creating the collateral, the design language across all the materials had to be uniform so that they would look like they belong together. The colors and fonts used had to be appropriate and relevant to the company and subject matter. The logo had to be designed in Illustrator, but the other materials had to be laid out in InDesign using the right format.
